Why I care

One day, as I was going to the beach in Vancouver, I see something floating in the ocean. I swim to it and see that it’s a plastic bottle! "At least there is only 1," I think. The wind blows and something hits me from behind. Oh no! It’s another plastic bottle! There's got to be some more around here. I turn around and see a whole mountain of them. The ocean was filled with the Plastic bottles, and it didn’t match the scene.
